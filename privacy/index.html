Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-21

1. Your files and input data

Every ThisIsMyPDF tool (PDF, image and calculator) runs entirely inside your browser. Files you select and values you enter are never transmitted to or stored on a server, and the operator has no way to access them. When you close the tab, any data being processed is gone.

2. Information we collect

This site has no user accounts and does not directly collect personal information such as names or contact details. To improve the service, we may collect anonymous visit statistics (page views, browser type, referral source, and similar non-identifying data).

3. Advertising and cookies

To keep the service free, this site may display third-party advertising (such as Google AdSense or Kakao AdFit). Advertising partners may use cookies to serve interest-based ads.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising in Google Ads Settings, and opt out of some third-party cookies at aboutads.info.
  • You can also block cookies in your browser settings (the site will keep working normally).

4. Theme preference

Your dark-mode choice is stored only in your browser (localStorage). It is never sent to a server.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the contact details on the About page.

6. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page along with its effective date.
