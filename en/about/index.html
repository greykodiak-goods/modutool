About ModuTool

ModuTool exists because we were tired of being asked to install software or create an account just to get a simple job done. Nobody should have to upload a contract to some company's server, then hunt for the real download button in a maze of ads, just to merge two PDFs. We built ModuTool to make that experience go away.

Our principles

  • Your files never touch a server. All processing finishes inside your browser. That means we have no server costs — which is why everything is free and unlimited.
  • No sign-up, no install. Open a tool and it should just work.
  • Ads stay out of the way. We run only the minimum advertising needed to cover running costs (like the domain), placed where it never interferes with the tools. We will never run fake download-button ads.
  • Get it right. Our calculators cite the formulas and regulations they are based on, right on the page.
