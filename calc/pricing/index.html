Why this pricing?

ThisIsMyCalculator never receives your files, so our server costs are close to zero. That's why we don't need "2 tasks per day" limits like other services. The basics stay free and unlimited; operating costs are covered by restrained ads and by Premium subscribers who prefer an ad-free, more convenient experience.

Frequently asked questions

Does Premium upload my files to a server?

No. On every plan, all file processing happens entirely inside your browser. Premium changes convenience features (no ads, batch processing) — never how your files are handled.

Will free features be reduced later?

No. Keeping today's free tools free and unlimited is an operating principle. Premium adds comfort on top — it never locks away the basics.
