Why is ThisIsMyIMG safe?

Most online converters upload your files to their servers for processing. ThisIsMyIMG works differently — using technology built into every modern browser (WebAssembly and Canvas), all processing happens entirely on your device. Contracts, ID scans and family photos never leave your computer, and everything stays fast even on a slow connection. No sign-up, no install, no file limits.
